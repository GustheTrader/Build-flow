# Business Process Mapping for AI Augmentation

## Construction Business Workflows

### Phase 1: Bidding & Estimation (AI Support: 70% automation potential)
**Human-in-the-Loop Points:**
- Initial project qualification and client verification
- Final bid approval and competitive pricing strategy
- Scope modifications and change order decisions

**Noesis Agent Applications:**
- **LangGraph Agent**: Analyze project requirements and cross-reference similar past projects
- **Memory Agent**: Store and retrieve historical project data, pricing patterns, client preferences
- **Validation Agent**: Check estimate completeness, compliance requirements, and feasibility
- **Minimax Agent**: Optimize pricing strategies based on competitor analysis and market conditions

## Human-in-the-Loop Decision Framework

### Risk-Based Decision Allocation
- **High-Risk Decisions (≥$50K impact)**: Human oversight required for final approval
- **Medium-Risk Decisions ($10K-$50K impact)**: AI recommendation with human review option
- **Low-Risk Decisions (<$10K impact)**: Full AI automation with monitoring

### Confidence-Based Routing
- **High AI Confidence (≥90%)**: Automated execution with post-action reporting
- **Medium AI Confidence (70-89%)**: Human notification with quick approval option
- **Low AI Confidence (<70%)**: Human review required before execution

### Continuous Learning Integration
- All human decisions become training data for the AI agents
- Regular model retraining based on actual outcomes vs. predictions
- Performance feedback loops to improve automation accuracy over time
